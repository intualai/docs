## Quickstart Guide

Welcome to the **IntualAI Quickstart Guide**! Follow these steps to get up and running with IntualAI quickly.

### Step 1: Create an Account
1. Visit the [IntualAI website](https://intual.ai).
2. Click on **Sign Up** and create your account.
3. Once registered, log in to the [IntualAI Dashboard](https://console.intual.ai).

### Step 2: Create an Organization
1. After logging in, navigate to the **Organizations** section in the dashboard.
2. Click on **Create Organization** and provide a name for your organization.
3. If you prefer, you can use the default organization that is automatically created for you.

### Step 3: Set Up Your First Project
1. Navigate to the **Projects** section in the dashboard.
2. Click on **Create Project** and provide a name and description for your project.
3. Configure your project settings and add team members as needed.

### Step 4: Explore Features
- Start creating workflows to automate your processes.
- Use our **Functions** for advanced decision-making and validation.
- Integrate with external tools like for seamless data processing.
